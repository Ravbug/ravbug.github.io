How to Play:

I have created a secret code of 4 colors! You must guess it.

Click the round pegs on the left column of the board to select their color. Then press Check if you think you have the correct answer.


If you have a pin in the correct position with the correct color, you will see a red pin on the right side of the board, as shown above.

If you have a pin with the correct color but incorrect position, you will see a white pin as shown above.

The placement of my hint pins does not correspond to which pin. That is for you to determine!

If you correctly guess my secret code within 10 attempts, you win!
